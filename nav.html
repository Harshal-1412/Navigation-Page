About Us

Empower Communities, Transform Lives

At Global Care, we believe in the power of community and the potential for every individual to lead a life of dignity and opportunity. Established in 2018, our mission is to create sustainable solutions that address the root causes of poverty, inequality, and social injustice.

Our Vision

A world where every person has the opportunity to achieve their fullest potential and contribute to a vibrant, inclusive, and just society.

Our Mission

To empower underprivileged communities by providing access to education, healthcare, and economic opportunities, fostering a spirit of self-reliance and community participation.

Education

We run various educational programs aimed at providing quality education to children and adults in marginalized communities. Our initiatives include:

  • After-School Tutoring: Helping students achieve academic success with extra support and resources.
  • Adult Literacy Classes: Enabling adults to learn to read and write, opening up new opportunities for employment and personal growth.
  • Scholarships: Providing financial assistance to deserving students for higher education.

Healthcare

Access to healthcare is a fundamental right. Our health programs focus on:

  • Mobile Health Clinics: Bringing medical services to remote and underserved areas.
  • Health Education Workshops: Educating communities on basic health practices and disease prevention.
  • Maternal and Child Health: Ensuring mothers and children receive the care they need for a healthy start in life.
